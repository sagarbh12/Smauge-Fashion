Return and Exchange Policy

1. Returns

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from Smauge Fashion Hub. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we accept returns within 30 days of the date of purchase. To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

Ensure the item is in its original condition, unworn, and with all tags attached. Include the original packing slip or order confirmation in the return package. Send an email to returns@smaugefashionhub.com with your order number, the reason for the return, and any relevant details. Once your return is approved, you will receive instructions on how to return the item. Please note that shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part.

2. Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item for a different size, color, or style, please follow the same steps as mentioned above for returns. In your email, clearly specify the details of the exchange, and we will do our best to accommodate your request, subject to product availability.

3. Defective or Incorrect Items

In the rare event that you receive a defective or incorrect item, please contact our customer support team at support@smaugefashionhub.com within 7 days of receiving the order. We will provide further instructions on how to return the item, and we will cover the shipping costs for the return.

4. Refunds

Refunds will be processed once we receive and inspect the returned items. Please allow 5-7 business days for the refund to be credited to the original payment method. Note that shipping costs are non-refundable.

5. Final Sale Items

Certain items may be marked as final sale and are not eligible for return or exchange. Please check the product description carefully before making a purchase.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Returns & Exchange Policy, feel free to contact us. We're here to assist you and ensure your shopping experience with Smauge Fashion Hub is enjoyable and stress-free.
